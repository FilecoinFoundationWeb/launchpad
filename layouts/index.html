Click on the video to learn more about the Launchpad journey!
Launchpad is a thriving hub for web3 learning and onboarding within the Protocol Labs Network, having welcomed over 200 learners all over the world! Whether newcomers or seasoned professionals, Launchpad has been instrumental in providing essential knowledge and support for individuals venturing into the exciting world of web3.
From training how to set up their first IPFS or Filecoin node, to fostering collaborative connections across the PLN, Launchpad has served as the catalyst for the growth and development of talented developers, marketing managers , recruiters, and many more professionals across the ecosystem. With our curriculum, you will be able to learn about Web3 at your own pace!

Launchpad is a training program covering topics like: blockchains, peer-to-peer communication, content addressing, and decentralized file storage.
The onboarding and training program was built over the span of 1.5 years, with 12 live cohorts held in various locations such as Paris, Reykjavik, and Palo Alto, just to name a few.
Launchpad has proven to be a valuable resource to over 270 participants from 20 diverse organizations.
